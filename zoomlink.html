Abstract

For the education world, Zoom has become the primary tool for holding classes and meeting with students as well as professors. This product is rich with tools and features that many are unaware how to use properly, and trying to attempt to perform these actions during class time can be time-consuming and embarrassing. Our product is geared towards making the Zoom experience easier. This will be acheived through USB device with a touch screen that has more features displayed, and buttons to control aspects like the camera and microphone.

The center is a touch screen that will have the User's most desired Zoom features. The red squares underneath are buttons that will activate a feature only while it is pressed, and the circles above the center are lights that correspond to the buttons.
